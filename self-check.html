Quick reflection tool

Self-check

This is not a diagnosis. It is a quick check-in to help someone think about whether their habits are starting to affect their life.

1. Do you often lose track of time while scrolling, gaming, or using gambling-related apps?

2. Have these habits affected your sleep, schoolwork, mood, money, or relationships?

3. Do you feel restless, stressed, or irritated when you cannot check your phone or place a bet?

4. Do algorithms or ads seem to keep showing you content that pulls you back into the habit?

5. Have you ever tried to cut back on scrolling, gambling, or screen time but struggled to stay consistent?

6. Do you sometimes open apps automatically without even thinking about it?

7. Have you ever hidden how much time or money you spent online, gambling, or scrolling?

8. Do you feel like certain apps or content control more of your attention than they should?

This tool is for awareness only. It is not medical advice or a professional diagnosis.
