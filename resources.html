Next steps

Resources

This page gives users simple next steps. The goal is to make getting help feel less intimidating.

Step 1

Track the habit

Write down when the behavior happens, what triggered it, and how it made you feel afterward.

Step 2

Set a boundary

Try app limits, deleting certain apps, unfollowing triggering content, or creating phone-free time.

Step 3

Talk to someone

Reach out to a friend, mentor, advisor, campus counseling center, or professional support resource.

Remove easy access

Delete apps, turn off notifications, or move certain apps off your home screen.

Tell one person

Let one trusted person know what you are trying to change so you are not doing it alone.
